Our Story

Established in 2010, Shree Modern Academy began with a vision to provide quality education to the children of Bheri-ganga and surrounding areas. What started as a small initiative with just 35 students has now grown into a reputable institution serving over 300 students from grades 1 to 7.

Our founder, Mr. Ram Bahadur Thapa, envisioned a school that would combine modern teaching methodologies with traditional values, creating an environment where children could thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and stimulating learning environment that empowers students to become confident, creative, and compassionate individuals who will contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in primary education, developing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Journey

2010

School Founded

Shree Modern Academy was established with just 35 students and 3 teachers in a small building.

2012

First Graduating Class

Our first batch of grade 5 students graduated, with 100% passing the district examinations.

2015

New Building Inaugurated

We moved to our current location with modern facilities and expanded to grade 7.

2018

Computer Lab Established

Introduced digital learning with a fully equipped computer lab and smart classrooms.

2022

Sports Complex Added

Expanded our facilities to include a playground and sports equipment for physical development.
