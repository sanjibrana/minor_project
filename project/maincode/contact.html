Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our school

Our regular school hours are from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM, Sunday through Friday. Saturday is a holiday. Extra-curricular activities may sometimes extend beyond these hours.

The admission process involves: 1) Inquiry and school visit, 2) Submission of application form with required documents, 3) Assessment for grades 2-7, and 4) Admission confirmation and fee payment.

You'll need: 1) Completed application form, 2) Birth certificate (for grade 1), 3) Transfer certificate (for grades 2-7), 4) Recent passport photos, and 5) Previous year's report card (for grades 2-7).

Currently, we don't provide school transportation. Parents are responsible for their children's commute to and from school.

We maintain a healthy teacher-student ratio of approximately 1:25 to ensure personalized attention for each student.
