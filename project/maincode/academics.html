Our Curriculum

At Shree Modern Academy, we follow a comprehensive curriculum that combines the National Curriculum with modern teaching methodologies. Our program is designed to develop critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills while ensuring strong foundational knowledge in all core subjects.

We emphasize holistic development through a balanced approach that includes academics, arts, physical education, and character development.

Grade Level Programs

Tailored learning experiences for each developmental stage

Primary Grade Program (Grades 1-3)

Our primary program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social skills through engaging, hands-on activities. We create a nurturing environment where young learners develop a love for learning.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Phonics-based reading and writing instruction
  • Basic mathematical concepts and problem-solving
  • Introduction to science and social studies
  • Creative arts and music
  • Social-emotional development

Lower Elementary Program (Grades 4-5)

Our lower elementary program builds on foundational skills while introducing more complex concepts across all subject areas. Students begin to develop greater independence and responsibility for their learning.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Advanced reading comprehension and writing skills
  • Mathematical operations and problem-solving
  • Scientific inquiry and experimentation
  • Nepal's history, geography, and culture
  • Introduction to computer literacy

Upper Elementary Program (Grades 6-7)

Our upper elementary program prepares students for secondary education with a rigorous academic curriculum that emphasizes critical thinking, research skills, and independent learning. Students engage in more complex projects and collaborative work.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Advanced literature analysis and composition
  • Algebraic concepts and geometry
  • Physical and life sciences
  • World history and civics
  • Computer applications and coding basics

Special Programs

Enriching experiences beyond the regular curriculum

Computer Education

Our computer lab is equipped with modern systems where students learn basic computer operations, typing, and introductory programming.

Library Program

Weekly library sessions encourage reading for pleasure and information, with a collection of over 1,000 books in English and Nepali.

Arts & Crafts

Creative expression through various media including painting, clay modeling, and traditional Nepali crafts.

Music Education

Students learn folk songs, play basic instruments, and develop an appreciation for both Nepali and Western music.

Physical Education

Regular sports activities including football, volleyball, and traditional Nepali games to promote fitness and teamwork.

Environmental Club

Students participate in tree planting, recycling programs, and learn about sustainable living practices.

Assessment & Evaluation

Tracking progress and supporting student growth

Continuous Assessment

Regular quizzes, classwork, and projects that provide ongoing feedback about student learning.

Term Examinations

Comprehensive tests at the end of each term to evaluate understanding of key concepts.

Project Work

Hands-on projects that demonstrate application of knowledge and develop research skills.

Parent-Teacher Meetings

Regular meetings to discuss student progress and strategies for improvement.
